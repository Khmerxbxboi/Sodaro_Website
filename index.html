About Sodaro Platform Application

Sodaro's AI-powered platform leverages a variety of data sources to provide accurate and insightful market analysis. The platform gathers real-time data, behavior patterns, news, events, and sentiment analysis to predict and assess market movements, making it an invaluable tool for investors.

By integrating these diverse data sets, Sodaro allows users to make data-driven decisions based on comprehensive market understanding. Whether you're interested in market trends, sentiment shifts, or real-time market feeds, Sodaro brings all of this data together in an easy-to-use interface.


Behavioral Data Analysis

Track global market news, human behavioral patterns, and economic data to predict market movements.


Goal-Oriented Trading

The application tailors its trading strategy, including swing and pattern trading, to help achieve that goal.

AI-Search Engine

Similar to how a search engine provides relevant results, this application continuously analyzes market data using AI.
